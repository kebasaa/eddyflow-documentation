# Biomet Data Processing and Advanced Features of EddyFlow

EddyFlow 4.0 Software presents many new features and processing options to the research community - improvements that meet the needs of a broad spectrum of users. In this webinar we introduce EddyFlow 4.0 – the latest iteration of our popular eddy covariance software. This version introduces many new features, including the integration of biological and meteorological (biomet) data from your eddy covariance site, outputs of ensemble spectra and co-spectra, automatic time lag optimization, and customizable planar fit tools.
