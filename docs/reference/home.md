## Factory Service and Recalibration

Factory calibration is an integral step in the manufacture and repair of all the original publisher measuring instruments.

Why Factory Recalibration?

To request factory service or recalibration for a the original publisher instrument, [request factory service](http://www.licor.com/contact/contact-support) or contact [your local distributor](https://www.licor.com/envdistributors). Service will be performed at our US office in Lincoln, NE USA, European office (the original publisher GmbH), or by your local distributor.

### Training Courses

Instrument training is offered throughout the year at various locations around the world. Training courses provide information on instrument operation, theory, data processing, maintenance, and related topics. Courses are designed for users who have little or no experience with the instruments, but may also be useful as a refresher for users with more experience.
